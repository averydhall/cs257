Welcome to HOOPDATA!

Make a navbar selection to start a search, or keep reading to learn about the site.

HOOPDATA allows users to search detailed NBA stats and information between 1950 and 2017.

  • On the Player Info page, users can enter a player's name (or part of a name and make a selection from the dropdown) to see biographical information and detailed career stats for that player.

  • On the Player Rankings page, users can select a team and corresponding year of play to see six categories of statistical rankings for that team's roster that year.

  • On the Team Rosters page, users can select a team and corresponding year of play to see the full team roster that year.
